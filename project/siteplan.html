Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Peakperformance.com

The name is a strong, effective domain choice for presenting the objetive of the page. It combines personal branding with clear terms, making it an excellent tool for building a professional online presence, attracting the right audience, and grow an online business.

Purpose

The purpouse is showcase and sell variety of supplements for athletes and sport people. Through this website, I aim to highlight the benefits of use natural supplements in a training plan. It serves as a central hub where I display diverse products, share insights through blog posts and tutorials, and connect with potential clients, employers, and collaborators. I seek to establish a strong personal brand, attract opportunities, and inspire others in the field.
